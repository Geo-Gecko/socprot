Shamim lives in Katanga, together with her six-month-old daughter, Fatuma. Shamim represents girls in Kampala aged 12 – 17 years who have given birth. Read her story from a day in her life.

ELODIE

Elodie lives in Katwe II, together with her two younger siblings - her 10-year-old sister Aminah and her eight-year-old brother Vinnie. They are refugees from the Democratic republic of Congo (DRC).
She represents female refugees in Kampala aged 13–17 years who are not attending school. Read her story from a day in her life.
